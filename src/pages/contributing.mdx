# Contributing

Thanks for being willing to contribute!

You can contribute as an organizing committee member or as a code contributor.

## Volunteering as an Organizing Committee Member

We strive to provide a friendly environment for developers to share and learn from each other, and you can help us to achieve that by volunteer as an committee members.

The roles and responsibilities of committee members are:

1. logistics (venue/food)
1. coordinations with speakers
1. emcee and session facilitation
1. participant registration & onboarding
1. event marketing
